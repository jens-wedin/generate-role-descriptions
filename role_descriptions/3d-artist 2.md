# 3D Artist

## Description
A 3D Artist is a digital artist who creates three-dimensional models, animations, and visual effects for a wide range of applications, including video games, films, television, virtual reality, and advertising. They are responsible for bringing characters, environments, and objects to life in a 3D space, ensuring they are visually appealing, technically sound, and aligned with the project's artistic vision.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Gaming, Entertainment, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Conceptualize and create high-polygon and low-polygon 3D models of characters, props, and environments.
- Create detailed textures and materials for 3D models, including UV mapping and shader setup.
- Implement lighting and rendering setups to create compelling visual scenes.
- Animate characters and objects to create dynamic and believable motion.
- Collaborate with art directors, designers, and engineers to ensure assets meet project requirements.
- Optimize 3D assets for real-time rendering and performance on various platforms.
- Rig characters and objects for animation.
- Stay current with industry trends, tools, and technologies in 3D art.

## Skills
- Proficiency in 3D modeling software such as Maya, 3ds Max, Blender, or ZBrush.
- Strong understanding of texturing and material creation using tools like Substance Painter and Photoshop.
- Knowledge of lighting, shading, and rendering techniques.
- Experience with game engines like Unreal Engine or Unity.
- Strong foundation in art fundamentals, including anatomy, composition, and color theory.
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
- Ability to work effectively in a collaborative team environment.
