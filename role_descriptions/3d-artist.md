# 3D Artist
## Description
Creates three-dimensional models, animations, and visual effects to be used in various digital media.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Gaming, Entertainment, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Create high-quality 3D models and textures
- Collaborate with animators and developers
- Optimize assets for performance
- Maintain consistent art style

## Skills
- Maya/Blender
- Texturing
- Lighting
- Rendering
