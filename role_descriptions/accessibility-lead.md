# Accessibility Lead
## Description
Leads efforts to ensure products and services are accessible to users of all abilities.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Government, Agency

## Responsibilities
- Audit products for WCAG compliance
- Train teams on accessible design principles
- Advocate for inclusive design practices
- Define accessibility roadmaps

## Skills
- WCAG
- Inclusive Design
- Screen Readers
- Mentorship
