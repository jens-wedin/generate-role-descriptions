# AI Prompt Designer

## Description
An AI Prompt Designer, also known as a Prompt Engineer, is a creative and analytical role focused on crafting high-quality inputs (prompts) for generative AI models to produce desired outputs. They act as a bridge between human intent and the AI's capabilities, meticulously designing and refining prompts to generate text, images, or code that is accurate, coherent, and contextually appropriate.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Creative Agency

## Responsibilities
- Design, write, and iterate on prompts for large language models (LLMs) and other generative AI systems.
- Develop and maintain a library of effective prompts for various use cases and scenarios.
- Analyze and evaluate the performance of different prompts and model responses.
- Collaborate with content creators, developers, and product managers to understand their needs and provide prompt engineering expertise.
- Stay abreast of the latest advancements in generative AI and prompt engineering techniques.
- Test for and mitigate potential biases and undesirable outputs from AI models.

## Skills
- Deep understanding of the capabilities and limitations of large language models (LLMs).
- Exceptional writing, editing, and communication skills.
- A logical and analytical mindset for systematic testing and refinement of prompts.
- Creativity and the ability to think outside the box to achieve desired AI outputs.
- Familiarity with different generative AI models and platforms.
- Basic understanding of programming concepts can be beneficial.
