# AI Prototyping Designer

## Description
An AI Prototyping Designer is a hands-on designer and technologist who rapidly builds functional prototypes to explore and validate ideas for AI-powered products and features. This role requires a blend of design thinking and technical skills to quickly bring concepts to life, enabling teams to test assumptions, gather user feedback, and demonstrate the potential of AI in a tangible way.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, R&D

## Responsibilities
- Utilize AI tools, APIs, and platforms to build interactive prototypes of AI-driven concepts.
- Work closely with product managers, designers, and engineers to quickly iterate on ideas and explore different approaches.
- Conduct rapid user testing with prototypes to gather feedback and validate product concepts.
- Stay current with the latest AI technologies and tools to identify new opportunities for innovation.
- Communicate and demonstrate the functionality and value of AI prototypes to stakeholders.
- Bridge the gap between design and engineering by creating prototypes that can inform technical development.

## Skills
- Proficiency in prototyping and creative coding tools (e.g., Framer, ProtoPie, JavaScript, Python).
- Experience working with APIs and integrating different services.
- A strong understanding of design principles and user-centered design methodologies.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and a bias for action.
- Ability to learn new technologies and tools quickly.
- Strong communication and presentation skills.
