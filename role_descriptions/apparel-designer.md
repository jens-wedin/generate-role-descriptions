# Apparel Designer
## Description
Designs clothing and accessories considering style, material, and production.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Fashion, Retail

## Responsibilities
- Sketch clothing concepts
- Select fabrics and trims
- Create technical packs for manufacturing
- Monitor fashion trends

## Skills
- Sketching
- Textiles
- Pattern Making
- Adobe Illustrator
