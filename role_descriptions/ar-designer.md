# AR Designer

## Description
An AR Designer, or Augmented Reality Designer, creates immersive and interactive experiences that blend the digital and physical worlds. This role involves designing and prototyping AR applications, from social media filters to complex industrial and educational tools. They need a unique combination of skills in 3D design, user experience (UX), and spatial computing to create intuitive and engaging augmented reality experiences.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Marketing, Gaming

## Responsibilities
- Design and conceptualize AR experiences, including user flows, interactions, and visual interfaces for spatial computing.
- Create 3D models, animations, and other assets for use in AR applications.
- Prototype and iterate on AR experiences using platforms like Unity, Unreal Engine, or AR-specific software like Spark AR and Lens Studio.
- Collaborate with developers to implement AR features and ensure a high-quality user experience.
- Conduct user testing and gather feedback to refine and improve AR experiences.
- Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in augmented reality.

## Skills
- Proficiency in 3D modeling and animation software (e.g., Blender, Maya, Cinema 4D).
- Experience with AR development platforms such as Unity, Unreal Engine, Spark AR, or Lens Studio.
- Strong understanding of user experience (UX) principles, particularly for spatial and 3D interfaces.
- A good sense of spatial awareness and the ability to design for the physical world.
- Creative thinking and problem-solving skills.
- Strong collaboration and communication skills.
