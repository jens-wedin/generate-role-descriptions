# Chief Digital Officer (CDO)
## Description
Executive driving digital transformation and the adoption of digital technologies.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Enterprise, Government

## Responsibilities
- Lead digital transformation initiatives
- Identify new digital opportunities
- Oversee digital product portfolio
- Drive cultural change towards digital

## Skills
- Digital Strategy
- Change Management
- Technology Trends
- Leadership
