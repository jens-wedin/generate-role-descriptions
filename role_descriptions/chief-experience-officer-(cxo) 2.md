# Chief Experience Officer (CXO)
## Description
Executive responsible for the overall customer experience across all touchpoints.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech, Retail, Service

## Responsibilities
- Own the end-to-end customer journey
- Unify CX efforts across departments
- Drive customer-centric strategy
- Measure and improve CX metrics

## Skills
- Customer Experience
- Operations
- Leadership
- Analytics
