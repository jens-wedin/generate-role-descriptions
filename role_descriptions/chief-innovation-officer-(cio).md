# Chief Innovation Officer (CIO)
## Description
Executive in charge of managing the process of innovation and change management.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Enterprise, Consulting

## Responsibilities
- Foster a culture of innovation
- Manage innovation portfolios
- Identify strategic partnerships
- Lead R&D initiatives

## Skills
- Innovation Management
- Strategic Foresight
- Leadership
- R&D
