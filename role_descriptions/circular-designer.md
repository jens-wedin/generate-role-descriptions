# Circular Designer
## Description
Designs products and systems with a focus on sustainability and the circular economy.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Manufacturing, Sustainability

## Responsibilities
- Design for disassembly and reuse
- Select sustainable materials
- Analyze product lifecycle
- Minimize waste in production

## Skills
- Systems Thinking
- Material Science
- Life Cycle Analysis
- Sustainability
