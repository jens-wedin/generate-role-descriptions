# Circular Designer

## Description
A Circular Designer is a forward-thinking professional who designs products, services, and systems based on the principles of the circular economy. Their goal is to eliminate waste and pollution, keep products and materials in use for as long as possible, and regenerate natural systems. They rethink the entire lifecycle of a product, from material selection to end-of-life, to create a more sustainable and restorative future.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Manufacturing, Sustainability

## Responsibilities
- Design products for durability, repairability, disassembly, and recyclability (Design for X).
- Research and select sustainable, renewable, and non-toxic materials.
- Conduct life cycle assessments (LCA) to understand the environmental impact of products and materials.
- Develop closed-loop systems and business models, such as product-as-a-service or take-back programs.
- Collaborate with engineers, supply chain specialists, and business strategists to implement circular design principles.
- Educate and advocate for circular design within the organization.

## Skills
- Deep understanding of circular economy principles and frameworks.
- Strong knowledge of sustainable materials and manufacturing processes.
- Experience with life cycle assessment (LCA) and other environmental impact assessment tools.
- Systems thinking and the ability to see the bigger picture.
- Creativity and a passion for sustainable innovation.
- Strong collaboration and communication skills.
- Knowledge of sustainable business models.
