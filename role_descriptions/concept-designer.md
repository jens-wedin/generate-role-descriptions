# Concept Designer

## Description
A Concept Designer, also known as a Concept Artist, is a creative visionary who specializes in the early stages of visual development. They are responsible for generating the initial ideas and visual direction for characters, environments, creatures, and props in industries like video games, film, and animation. They are masters of visual brainstorming, quickly exploring a wide range of ideas to find the most compelling and innovative solutions.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Entertainment, Gaming, Product

## Responsibilities
- Brainstorm and generate a wide variety of ideas and sketches based on project briefs and art direction.
- Create detailed concept art that establishes the mood, tone, and visual style of a project.
- Design characters, environments, and props that are both visually appealing and functional for the needs of the project.
- Collaborate with art directors, writers, and other designers to develop and refine the visual world of a project.
- Iterate on designs based on feedback and creative direction.
- Produce high-quality, polished concept art for presentation and production purposes.

## Skills
- Exceptional drawing, painting, and illustration skills.
- Strong understanding of art fundamentals, including composition, color theory, lighting, and perspective.
- Ability to work in a variety of styles and genres.
- Proficiency in digital art software such as Photoshop, Procreate, or Painter.
- A strong imagination and a passion for visual storytelling.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong sense of creativity and visual problem-solving.
