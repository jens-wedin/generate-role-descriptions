# Concept Designer
## Description
Visualizes initial ideas for characters, environments, or products.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Entertainment, Gaming, Product

## Responsibilities
- Create rapid sketches and iterations
- Establish visual direction
- Collaborate with art directors
- Produce polished concept art
