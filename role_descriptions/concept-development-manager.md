# Concept Development Manager

## Description
A Concept Development Manager is a strategic leader who oversees the fuzzy front end of the innovation process. They are responsible for managing the journey of a new idea, from initial spark to a fully validated concept ready for development. They create the conditions for creativity and collaboration, while also bringing a structured and disciplined approach to the often-chaotic process of innovation.

## Org Level
Manager

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Retail, FMCG

## Responsibilities
- Lead and facilitate ideation and brainstorming workshops to generate a strong pipeline of new ideas.
- Develop and manage a process for evaluating, prioritizing, and selecting the most promising concepts.
- Lead cross-functional teams in the development and validation of new concepts.
- Oversee the creation of prototypes, mockups, and other tools to test and learn about new concepts.
- Manage the budget, timeline, and resources for concept development projects.
- Collaborate with R&D, marketing, and sales to ensure that new concepts are desirable, feasible, and viable.
- Present and pitch new concepts to senior leadership for funding and approval.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Expertise in innovation methodologies, such as design thinking, lean startup, and jobs-to-be-done.
- Excellent facilitation and communication skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- Experience with project management and a proven ability to deliver results.
- A passion for innovation and a creative mindset.
- Ability to navigate ambiguity and uncertainty.
