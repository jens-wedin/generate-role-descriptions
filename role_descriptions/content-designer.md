# Content Designer

## Description
A Content Designer is a user experience professional who focuses on the content of a product or service. They are responsible for creating clear, concise, and user-focused content that helps people achieve their goals. They are advocates for the user, ensuring that the language and structure of the content is intuitive, accessible, and easy to understand.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Government

## Responsibilities
- Write, edit, and structure content for user interfaces, websites, and other digital products.
- Develop and maintain content strategies, guidelines, and style guides.
- Collaborate with UX designers, researchers, and product managers to create a cohesive and user-centered experience.
- Use data and user research to inform content decisions.
- Conduct content audits and inventories to identify gaps and opportunities for improvement.
- Champion the importance of clear and accessible language throughout the organization.

## Skills
- Exceptional writing, editing, and proofreading skills.
- A deep understanding of user experience (UX) and user-centered design principles.
- Experience with content strategy and information architecture.
- Knowledge of accessibility best practices for content.
- Strong communication and collaboration skills.
- Ability to translate complex concepts into simple and clear language.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong understanding of UX writing and content design.
