# Context Designer
## Description
Designs for the situational context in which a product or service is used.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
IoT, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Map user contexts and environments
- Design adaptive experiences
- Consider physical and social factors
- Research usage scenarios

## Skills
- Contextual Inquiry
- Systems Thinking
- UX Research
- IOT
