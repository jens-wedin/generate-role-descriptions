# Conversation Designer

## Description
A Conversation Designer is a user experience professional who specializes in designing the interactions for chatbots, voice assistants, and other conversational AI systems. They are the architects of the conversation, crafting the flow, logic, and personality of the AI to create a natural, intuitive, and engaging user experience. They are part writer, part designer, and part linguist, with a deep understanding of how humans communicate.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, AI

## Responsibilities
- Design and write the dialogue and conversational flows for chatbots and voice assistants.
- Define the persona, tone of voice, and personality of the conversational AI.
- Create user journey maps, conversation flows, and other design artifacts.
- Collaborate with developers and data scientists to train and improve the AI's natural language understanding (NLU).
- Conduct user research and usability testing to gather feedback and iterate on the conversational experience.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in conversational AI.

## Skills
- Exceptional writing, editing, and communication skills.
- A deep understanding of user experience (UX) and user-centered design principles.
- Knowledge of linguistics and the principles of human conversation.
- Experience with conversational design tools and platforms.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- A passion for creating natural and engaging conversational experiences.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong understanding of conversational design.
