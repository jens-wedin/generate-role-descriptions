# Creative Team Manager

## Description
A Creative Team Manager is an operational and people leader who is responsible for the day-to-day management of a team of creative professionals. They are the glue that holds the creative team together, ensuring that projects are delivered on time, on budget, and to a high standard of quality. They are master organizers, communicators, and problem-solvers, with a deep understanding of the creative process.

## Org Level
Manager

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Agency, In-house

## Responsibilities
- Manage the workflow and resources of the creative team, including designers, writers, and other creative professionals.
- Develop and manage project timelines, budgets, and deliverables.
- Act as the primary point of contact for clients and internal stakeholders.
- Facilitate communication and collaboration within the creative team and with other departments.
- Mentor and develop the skills of the creative team members.
- Ensure that all creative work is on-brand and meets a high standard of quality.
- Continuously improve the creative process and workflow.

## Skills
- Strong project management and organizational skills.
- Excellent communication, interpersonal, and leadership skills.
- A deep understanding of the creative process and the needs of creative professionals.
- Experience with project management software and other tools.
- Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities in a fast-paced environment.
- A passion for creativity and a commitment to quality.
- Experience in a creative role, such as a designer or writer, is often a plus.
