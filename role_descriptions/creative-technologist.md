# Creative Technologist

## Description
A Creative Technologist is a hybrid professional who bridges the gap between creativity and technology. They are part designer, part developer, and part strategist, with a passion for using emerging technologies to create innovative and engaging experiences. They are master prototypers, experimenters, and problem-solvers, who are comfortable working in a fast-paced and ambiguous environment.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Agency, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Explore and experiment with new and emerging technologies, such as AR/VR, AI, and physical computing.
- Rapidly prototype and build interactive experiences to demonstrate the potential of new ideas.
- Collaborate with designers, developers, and strategists to bring creative concepts to life.
- Develop and build interactive installations, prototypes, and other creative technology projects.
- Act as a bridge between the design and engineering teams, translating creative ideas into technical requirements.
- Inspire and educate others about the possibilities of creative technology.

## Skills
- Strong creative and design thinking skills.
- Proficiency in creative coding frameworks and languages, such as JavaScript, Python, Processing, or openFrameworks.
- Experience with emerging technologies, such as AR/VR, AI, and physical computing.
- A passion for prototyping and experimentation.
- Excellent problem-solving and collaboration skills.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong understanding of creative technology and a passion for innovation.
