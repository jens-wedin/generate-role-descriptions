# CX Architect

## Description
A CX Architect, or Customer Experience Architect, is a strategic designer who is responsible for the overall structure and design of the customer experience ecosystem. They take a holistic view of the customer journey, mapping out all the touchpoints and interactions a customer has with a company. They are systems thinkers who design the blueprint for a seamless, consistent, and delightful customer experience.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Enterprise, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Map and analyze the end-to-end customer journey across all channels and touchpoints.
- Design and develop a comprehensive CX architecture that aligns with the company's brand and business goals.
- Identify and prioritize key opportunities for improving the customer experience.
- Collaborate with product, marketing, sales, and support teams to ensure a cohesive and integrated customer experience.
- Develop and maintain service blueprints, journey maps, and other CX design artifacts.
- Establish and govern CX standards and guidelines.

## Skills
- Expertise in service design, systems thinking, and user experience design.
- Strong analytical and strategic thinking skills.
- A deep understanding of the customer journey and the factors that influence customer satisfaction and loyalty.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and facilitation skills.
- Experience with CX design tools and methodologies, such as service blueprints and journey mapping.
- A passion for creating seamless and customer-centric experiences.
