# CX Lead

## Description
A CX Lead, or Customer Experience Lead, is a senior-level professional who is responsible for leading and guiding a team of CX designers and researchers. They are a player-coach who is both a skilled CX practitioner and a mentor to others. They are responsible for setting the vision and direction for the CX team, and for ensuring that the team is delivering high-quality work that meets the needs of both customers and the business.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech, Service

## Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor a team of CX designers and researchers.
- Develop and implement a CX strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Oversee the design and execution of CX projects from concept to completion.
- Champion a customer-centric culture throughout the organization.
- Collaborate with product, engineering, and other teams to ensure a cohesive and consistent customer experience.
- Establish and track key CX metrics to measure the success of the team's work.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in CX.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Expertise in customer experience, service design, and user research.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and facilitation skills.
- A data-driven mindset and a focus on results.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing teams.
- A strong portfolio that demonstrates a deep understanding of CX and a track record of success.
