# CX Specialist

## Description
A CX Specialist, or Customer Experience Specialist, is a professional who is focused on the analysis and improvement of the customer experience. They are data-driven and customer-obsessed, with a passion for identifying and solving customer problems. They work closely with customer support, product, and other teams to ensure that the voice of the customer is heard and acted upon.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Customer Support, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Analyze customer feedback from a variety of sources, such as surveys (NPS, CSAT), support tickets, and social media.
- Identify and prioritize key pain points and friction points in the customer journey.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement solutions to customer problems.
- Track and measure the impact of CX initiatives on key metrics.
- Create and maintain reports and dashboards to communicate customer insights to stakeholders.
- Act as a voice for the customer within the organization.

## Skills
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Experience with data analysis and visualization tools.
- A deep understanding of customer service and support processes.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A passion for understanding and helping customers.
- Experience with CRM and customer feedback management software.
