# Design Coach
## Description
Mentors and trains individuals or teams in design thinking and practices.

## Org Level

## Medium

## Industry

## Responsibilities
- Conduct training workshops
- Mentor designers one-on-one
- Help teams improve processes
- Facilitate design sprints
