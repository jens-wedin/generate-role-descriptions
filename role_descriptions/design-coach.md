# Design Coach

## Description
A Design Coach is an experienced design professional who is dedicated to helping individuals and teams grow their design skills and capabilities. They are a mentor, a facilitator, and a guide, with a passion for helping others reach their full potential. They work with designers at all levels, from junior to senior, to help them improve their craft, their process, and their impact.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Consulting, Education

## Responsibilities
- Provide one-on-one coaching and mentoring to designers.
- Facilitate workshops and training sessions on a variety of design topics, such as design thinking, user research, and prototyping.
- Help design teams improve their processes and workflows.
- Facilitate design sprints and other collaborative design activities.
- Provide feedback and guidance on design work.
- Help designers develop their careers and achieve their professional goals.

## Skills
- Deep expertise in a variety of design disciplines, such as UX design, service design, or product design.
- Strong coaching, mentoring, and facilitation skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- A passion for helping others learn and grow.
- A patient and empathetic approach to coaching.
- Experience in a senior design role is often a plus.
