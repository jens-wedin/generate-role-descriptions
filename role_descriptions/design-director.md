# Design Director

## Description
A Design Director is a senior creative leader who is responsible for the overall design vision and execution for a company, a product, or a portfolio of projects. They are a visionary and a mentor, with a passion for building and leading high-performing design teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for design excellence and for ensuring that the design team is creating work that is both beautiful and effective.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Agency, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the design vision and strategy for the organization.
- Lead and mentor a team of designers, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the design of all products, services, and experiences, ensuring a high standard of quality and consistency.
- Collaborate with product, engineering, and other teams to ensure that design is integrated into the entire product development process.
- Champion a user-centered design culture throughout the organization.
- Attract, hire, and retain top design talent.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in design.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- A deep expertise in a variety of design disciplines, such as UX design, product design, or brand design.
- A compelling portfolio of work that demonstrates a commitment to design excellence.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing design teams.
