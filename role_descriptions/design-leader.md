# Design Leader

## Description
A Design Leader is a senior design professional who is responsible for the overall design strategy, vision, and culture for an organization. They are a visionary and an advocate for design, with a passion for building and leading high-performing design teams. They are responsible for ensuring that design has a seat at the table and for using design to drive business success.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech, Enterprise

## Responsibilities
- Develop and champion a clear and compelling design vision and strategy for the organization.
- Build and lead a high-performing design team, including designers, researchers, and other design professionals.
- Foster a strong and healthy design culture that values creativity, collaboration, and user-centricity.
- Advocate for the value of design to senior leadership and other stakeholders.
- Align the design strategy with the overall business strategy.
- Drive continuous improvement in the design team's processes and practices.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and vision-setting skills.
- Deep expertise in design, business, and technology.
- Excellent communication, storytelling, and influencing skills.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing teams.
- A proven track record of using design to drive business results.
- Experience in a senior design leadership role.
