# Design Strategist

## Description
A Design Strategist is a hybrid professional who combines design thinking with business strategy to solve complex challenges and drive innovation. They work at the intersection of design, business, and technology, using a human-centered approach to identify new opportunities and create a clear and actionable path forward. They are big-picture thinkers who are skilled at both divergent and convergent thinking.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Consulting, Innovation

## Responsibilities
- Lead and facilitate the strategic design process, from research and insights to strategy and execution.
- Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and user research to identify opportunities for innovation.
- Develop and articulate a clear and compelling strategic vision for new products, services, and experiences.
- Create strategic roadmaps, business cases, and other deliverables to communicate the strategy to stakeholders.
- Facilitate workshops and collaborative sessions with cross-functional teams and senior leadership.
- Act as a bridge between the design team and the business, ensuring that design is aligned with the company's strategic goals.

## Skills
- Expertise in design thinking, service design, and other strategic design methodologies.
- Strong business acumen and a deep understanding of business strategy.
- Excellent analytical, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
- Strong communication, facilitation, and storytelling skills.
- A passion for innovation and a creative mindset.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to use design to solve complex business challenges.
