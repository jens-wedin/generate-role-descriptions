# Design Technologist

## Description
A Design Technologist is a creative professional who is passionate about the intersection of design and technology. They are a designer who can code, and a developer who has a deep understanding of design. They are responsible for exploring and prototyping new ideas, pushing the boundaries of what is possible with technology. They are a bridge between the design and engineering teams, helping to ensure that the final product is both innovative and technically feasible.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Agency

## Responsibilities
- Explore and experiment with new and emerging technologies, such as AR/VR, AI, and creative coding.
- Build functional prototypes to demonstrate and validate new design concepts.
- Collaborate with designers and engineers to bring innovative ideas to life.
- Provide technical guidance and support to the design team.
- Contribute to the development of design systems and other tools to improve the design process.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in design and technology.

## Skills
- Strong creative and design thinking skills.
- Proficiency in a variety of programming languages and frameworks, such as JavaScript, Python, and React.
- Experience with creative coding tools and libraries, such as Processing, openFrameworks, or p5.js.
- A passion for prototyping and experimentation.
- Excellent problem-solving and collaboration skills.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong ability to use technology to create innovative and engaging experiences.
