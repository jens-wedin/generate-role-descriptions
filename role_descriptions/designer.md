# Designer

## Description
A Designer is a creative professional who is responsible for the visual and functional aspects of a product, service, or experience. They are problem-solvers who use their skills in a variety of design disciplines to create solutions that are both beautiful and effective. They are empathetic and user-centered, with a passion for creating experiences that are both enjoyable and easy to use.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
General

## Responsibilities
- Create and execute designs for a variety of projects, from websites and mobile apps to branding and marketing materials.
- Collaborate with other designers, product managers, engineers, and other stakeholders to bring designs to life.
- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions.
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and other design artifacts to communicate design concepts.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in design.
- Advocate for the user and for design best practices.

## Skills
- Strong skills in a variety of design disciplines, such as UX design, visual design, or interaction design.
- Proficiency in design and prototyping tools, such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe Creative Suite.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A user-centered mindset and a passion for creating great experiences.
