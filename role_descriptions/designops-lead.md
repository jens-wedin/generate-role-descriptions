# DesignOps Lead

## Description
A DesignOps Lead is a strategic and operational leader who is responsible for the efficiency and effectiveness of a design organization. They are the backbone of the design team, responsible for creating the systems, processes, and tools that enable designers to do their best work. They are master organizers, problem-solvers, and collaborators, with a passion for making design teams more productive and impactful.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech, Enterprise

## Responsibilities
- Design and implement the operational strategy for the design organization.
- Optimize the design team's workflows and processes.
- Manage the design team's tools, systems, and budget.
- Develop and manage programs to support the design team, such as onboarding, training, and career development.
- Measure and track the impact of the design team's work.
- Foster a strong and healthy design culture.
- Act as a bridge between the design team and other departments.

## Skills
- Strong operational and project management skills.
- A deep understanding of the design process and the needs of design teams.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and influencing skills.
- A data-driven mindset and a focus on continuous improvement.
- Experience with a variety of design tools and technologies.
- A passion for making design teams more effective and efficient.
