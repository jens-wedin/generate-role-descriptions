# Digital Design Director

## Description
A Digital Design Director is a senior creative leader who is responsible for the overall digital design vision and execution for a company, a brand, or a portfolio of clients. They are a visionary and a mentor, with a passion for building and leading high-performing digital design teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for digital design excellence and for ensuring that the design team is creating work that is both innovative and effective.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Agency, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the digital design vision and strategy for the organization.
- Lead and mentor a team of digital designers, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the design of all digital products, services, and experiences, ensuring a high standard of quality and consistency.
- Collaborate with other creative leaders, as well as with product, engineering, and marketing teams, to ensure a cohesive and integrated brand experience.
- Champion a user-centered and data-informed design culture.
- Attract, hire, and retain top digital design talent.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in digital design.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Deep expertise in a variety of digital design disciplines, such as web design, mobile design, and interaction design.
- A compelling portfolio of work that demonstrates a commitment to digital design excellence.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing digital design teams.
