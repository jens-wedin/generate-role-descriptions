# Digital Designer

## Description
A Digital Designer is a creative professional who specializes in designing for digital platforms and media. They are responsible for creating a wide range of visual assets, from websites and mobile apps to social media graphics and online ads. They are masters of the digital canvas, with a deep understanding of how to create designs that are not only beautiful, but also user-friendly and effective.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Agency, Tech, Marketing

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of digital assets, including websites, mobile apps, social media graphics, and online ads.
- Collaborate with other designers, developers, and marketers to bring digital projects to life.
- Ensure that all digital designs are on-brand and consistent with the company's visual identity.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in digital design.
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and other design artifacts to communicate design concepts.
- Advocate for the user and for design best practices.

## Skills
- Strong skills in a variety of digital design disciplines, such as web design, mobile design, or interaction design.
- Proficiency in design and prototyping tools, such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe Creative Suite.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of digital design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A user-centered mindset and a passion for creating great digital experiences.
