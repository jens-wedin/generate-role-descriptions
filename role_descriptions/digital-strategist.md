# Digital Strategist

## Description
A Digital Strategist is a forward-thinking professional who is responsible for developing and executing the digital strategy for a company or a brand. They are experts in the digital landscape, with a deep understanding of how to use digital channels and technologies to achieve business goals. They are analytical and creative thinkers, with a passion for using data to drive results.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Marketing, Consulting

## Responsibilities
- Develop and execute a comprehensive digital strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Conduct research and analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and threats in the digital landscape.
- Develop and manage digital marketing campaigns across a variety of channels, such as SEO, SEM, social media, and email.
- Analyze and report on the performance of digital marketing campaigns.
- Identify and evaluate new and emerging digital technologies.
- Collaborate with other teams, such as marketing, sales, and product, to ensure a cohesive and integrated digital presence.

## Skills
- Expertise in digital marketing, including SEO, SEM, social media, and email marketing.
- Strong analytical and data-driven decision-making skills.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
- A passion for the digital world and a deep understanding of the latest trends and technologies.
- A strategic and creative mindset.
- Experience with a variety of digital marketing tools and technologies.
