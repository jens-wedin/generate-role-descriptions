# Director of Product Design
## Description
Leads the product design discipline and aligns it with product strategy.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, SaaS

## Responsibilities
- Align design with product roadmap
- Manage design managers
- Ensure product quality
- Drive user-centricity
