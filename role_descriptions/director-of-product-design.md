# Director of Product Design

## Description
A Director of Product Design is a senior leader who is responsible for the overall product design vision, strategy, and execution for a company or a product line. They are a visionary and a mentor, with a passion for building and leading high-performing product design teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for product design excellence and for ensuring that the design team is creating products that are both user-friendly and valuable to the business.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, SaaS

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the product design vision and strategy.
- Lead and mentor a team of product designers and design managers, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the design of all products, ensuring a high standard of quality, consistency, and user-friendliness.
- Collaborate with other leaders in product, engineering, and marketing to ensure a cohesive and integrated product experience.
- Champion a user-centered and data-informed design culture.
- Attract, hire, and retain top product design talent.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in product design.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Deep expertise in product design, UX design, and user research.
- A compelling portfolio of work that demonstrates a commitment to product design excellence.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing product design teams.
