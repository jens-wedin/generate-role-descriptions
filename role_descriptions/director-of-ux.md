# Director of UX

## Description
A Director of UX is a senior leader who is responsible for the overall user experience (UX) vision, strategy, and execution for a company. They are a visionary and a mentor, with a passion for building and leading high-performing UX teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for UX excellence and for ensuring that the UX team is creating products that are both user-friendly and valuable to the business.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Enterprise

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the UX vision and strategy for the organization.
- Lead and mentor a team of UX designers, researchers, and other UX professionals, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the UX of all products, ensuring a high standard of quality, consistency, and usability.
- Collaborate with other leaders in product, engineering, and marketing to ensure a cohesive and integrated user experience.
- Champion a user-centered design culture throughout the organization.
- Attract, hire, and retain top UX talent.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in UX.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Deep expertise in UX design, user research, and interaction design.
- A compelling portfolio of work that demonstrates a commitment to UX excellence.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing UX teams.
