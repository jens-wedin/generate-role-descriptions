# Email Designer

## Description
An Email Designer is a creative professional who specializes in designing and coding email marketing campaigns. They are responsible for creating emails that are not only visually appealing, but also responsive, accessible, and optimized for conversion. They are a hybrid of a designer and a developer, with a deep understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities of the email channel.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Marketing, E-commerce

## Responsibilities
- Design and code responsive and accessible HTML email templates.
- Create visually engaging email designs that are aligned with the company's brand and marketing goals.
- Test emails across a variety of email clients and devices to ensure a consistent and high-quality experience.
- Collaborate with marketers and copywriters to develop and execute email marketing campaigns.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in email design and development.
- Optimize emails for deliverability, open rates, and click-through rates.

## Skills
- Expertise in HTML and CSS for email.
- Strong graphic design skills and a keen eye for detail.
- Experience with email marketing platforms and testing tools, such as Litmus or Email on Acid.
- A deep understanding of email marketing best practices, including responsive design, accessibility, and deliverability.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to design and code effective email campaigns.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
