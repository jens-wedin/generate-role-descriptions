# Environment Artist

## Description
An Environment Artist is a 3D artist who specializes in creating the worlds and settings for video games, films, and other digital media. They are responsible for building everything from vast landscapes and futuristic cities to detailed interior spaces. They are world-builders who use their artistic and technical skills to create immersive and believable environments that tell a story and draw the viewer in.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Gaming, Film

## Responsibilities
- Model and texture 3D environments, including terrain, buildings, and props.
- Create and place assets in a game engine or 3D software to build out the environment.
- Set up lighting and atmospheric effects to create a specific mood and tone.
- Optimize environments for performance and memory usage.
- Collaborate with level designers, concept artists, and other team members to create a cohesive and immersive world.
- Stay current with the latest trends and techniques in 3D environment art.

## Skills
- Expertise in 3D modeling and texturing software, such as Maya, 3ds Max, or Blender.
- Experience with game engines, such as Unreal Engine or Unity.
- Strong understanding of art fundamentals, including composition, color theory, and lighting.
- A keen eye for detail and a passion for creating immersive worlds.
- Excellent problem-solving and collaboration skills.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong ability to create high-quality 3D environments.
