# Ergonomics Designer
## Description
Designs products and spaces to fit human physical capabilities.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Industrial, Automotive

## Responsibilities
- Analyze human factors
- Test physical prototypes
- Ensure safety and comfort
- Apply anthropometric data

- Industrial Design
- Safety Standards
- Testing
