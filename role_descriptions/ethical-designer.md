# Ethical Designer

## Description
An Ethical Designer is a design professional who is committed to creating products, services, and experiences that are not only user-friendly, but also responsible, fair, and just. They are critical thinkers who are passionate about using their design skills to make a positive impact on the world. They are the conscience of the design team, advocating for the rights and well-being of users and society as a whole.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech, Social Impact

## Responsibilities
- Identify and assess the ethical risks and implications of design decisions.
- Advocate for user privacy, data protection, and other ethical considerations.
- Design for inclusivity, diversity, and accessibility.
- Actively work to prevent the creation of dark patterns and other manipulative design practices.
- Collaborate with other designers, product managers, engineers, and other stakeholders to embed ethical considerations into the design process.
- Stay current with the latest research and best practices in ethical design.

## Skills
- A deep understanding of ethical principles and frameworks.
- Strong critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and advocacy skills.
- A passion for creating responsible, fair, and just products and services.
- A commitment to lifelong learning and continuous improvement.
- Experience in a variety of design disciplines is often a plus.
