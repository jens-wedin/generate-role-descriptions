# Exhibition Designer

## Description
An Exhibition Designer is a creative professional who specializes in designing and creating immersive and engaging experiences for exhibitions, museums, trade shows, and other public spaces. They are storytellers who use a variety of design disciplines, from spatial and graphic design to interactive media, to communicate a message and create a memorable experience for visitors.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Museums, Events

## Responsibilities
- Develop and execute the overall design concept for an exhibition.
- Design the spatial layout and flow of the exhibition space.
- Design and create custom displays, fixtures, and interactive installations.
- Collaborate with curators, content developers, and other stakeholders to ensure a cohesive and compelling narrative.
- Oversee the production and installation of all exhibition elements.
- Manage the budget and timeline for the design and production of the exhibition.

## Skills
- Strong spatial and 3D design skills.
- Proficiency in design software, such as SketchUp, AutoCAD, and Adobe Creative Suite.
- A deep understanding of storytelling and narrative design.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and project management skills.
- A passion for creating immersive and engaging experiences.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong ability to design and execute exhibitions.
