# Experience Lead

## Description
An Experience Lead is a senior-level professional who is responsible for leading and guiding a team of experience designers and researchers. They are a player-coach who is both a skilled experience design practitioner and a mentor to others. They are responsible for setting the vision and direction for the experience design team, and for ensuring that the team is delivering high-quality work that meets the needs of both users and the business.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Agency

## Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor a team of experience designers and researchers.
- Develop and implement an experience design strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Oversee the design and execution of experience design projects from concept to completion.
- Champion a human-centered design culture throughout the organization.
- Collaborate with product, engineering, and other teams to ensure a cohesive and consistent user experience.
- Establish and track key experience design metrics to measure the success of the team's work.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in experience design.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Expertise in experience design, user research, and interaction design.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and facilitation skills.
- A data-driven mindset and a focus on results.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing teams.
- A strong portfolio that demonstrates a deep understanding of experience design and a track record of success.
