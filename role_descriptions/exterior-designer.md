# Exterior Designer
## Description
Designs the outer appearance of cars, buildings, or products.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry

## Responsibilities
- Sketch exterior concepts
- Create 3D surfaces (CAS)
- Refine aesthetics and aero
- Collaborate with engineers
