# Exterior Designer

## Description
An Exterior Designer is a creative professional who specializes in the design of the exterior of products, vehicles, and buildings. They are responsible for the overall aesthetic and form of the exterior, ensuring that it is not only visually appealing, but also functional and manufacturable. They are sculptors of form, with a deep understanding of materials, manufacturing processes, and the emotional impact of design.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Automotive, Architecture

## Responsibilities
- Develop and sketch a wide range of exterior design concepts.
- Create detailed 3D models and renderings of exterior designs.
- Collaborate with engineers, aerodynamicists, and other specialists to ensure that the exterior design is both beautiful and functional.
- Select and specify materials and finishes for the exterior.
- Create and present design concepts to clients and internal stakeholders.
- Stay current with the latest trends and technologies in exterior design.

## Skills
- Exceptional sketching and 3D modeling skills.
- A deep understanding of form, proportion, and surface development.
- Expertise in 3D modeling and rendering software, such as Alias, Rhino, or VRED.
- A strong sense of aesthetics and a passion for creating beautiful and innovative designs.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong ability to create compelling and innovative exterior designs.
