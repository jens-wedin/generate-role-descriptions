# Generative Designer

## Description
A Generative Designer is a creative professional who uses algorithms, code, and other computational tools to create designs. They are a hybrid of a designer and a programmer, with a passion for using technology to explore new creative possibilities. They are experts in creating systems that can generate a wide range of design variations, often in response to data or user input.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Architecture, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop generative systems and algorithms.
- Use code and other computational tools to create a wide range of design outputs, from graphics and animations to 3D models and architectural forms.
- Collaborate with other designers, artists, and engineers to integrate generative techniques into creative projects.
- Explore and experiment with new and emerging technologies in the field of generative design.
- Curate and select the most compelling outputs from generative systems.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in generative art and design.

## Skills
- Expertise in a variety of programming languages and creative coding frameworks, such as JavaScript, Python, Processing, or openFrameworks.
- A strong understanding of design principles, including typography, color, and composition.
- A passion for experimentation and a creative mindset.
- Excellent problem-solving and collaboration skills.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong ability to use code to create innovative and compelling designs.
