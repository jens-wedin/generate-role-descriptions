# Graphic Designer
## Description
Creates visual concepts to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
General

## Responsibilities
- Develop graphics for product illustrations, logos, and websites
- Select colors, images, text style, and layout
- Review designs for errors before printing or publishing
- Collaborate with copywriters and creative directors

## Skills
- Adobe Creative Suite
- Typography
- Layout
- Creativity
