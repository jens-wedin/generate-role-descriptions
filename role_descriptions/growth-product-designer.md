# Growth Product Designer

## Description
A Growth Product Designer is a user-centered design professional who is focused on driving business growth through the design of products and experiences. They are a hybrid of a product designer, a marketer, and a data analyst, with a deep understanding of how to use design to acquire, activate, and retain users. They are passionate about experimentation and are constantly looking for new and innovative ways to grow the business.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, SaaS

## Responsibilities
- Design and conduct experiments to test and validate new growth hypotheses.
- Use data and analytics to identify opportunities for growth and to measure the impact of design changes.
- Design and prototype new features and experiences that are designed to drive user acquisition, activation, and retention.
- Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and marketers to execute on growth initiatives.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in growth design and experimentation.
- Advocate for a data-informed and experimental approach to design.

## Skills
- A deep understanding of user-centered design principles and methodologies.
- Expertise in A/B testing and other experimentation methods.
- Strong analytical and data-driven decision-making skills.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and storytelling skills.
- A passion for growth and a creative mindset.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to use design to drive business results.
