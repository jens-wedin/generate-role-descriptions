# Head of Customer Experience

## Description
A Head of Customer Experience is a senior leader who is responsible for the overall customer experience (CX) vision, strategy, and execution for a company. They are the ultimate advocate for the customer, with a passion for building and leading high-performing CX teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for CX excellence and for ensuring that the CX team is creating end-to-end experiences that are both user-friendly and valuable to the business.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Service, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the CX vision and strategy for the organization.
- Lead and mentor a team of CX professionals, including designers, researchers, and strategists, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the design and implementation of all CX initiatives, ensuring a high standard of quality and consistency.
- Collaborate with other leaders in product, marketing, and operations to ensure a cohesive and integrated customer experience.
- Champion a customer-centric culture throughout the organization.
- Attract, hire, and retain top CX talent.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in CX.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Deep expertise in customer experience, service design, and user research.
- A compelling track record of improving customer satisfaction and loyalty.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing CX teams.
