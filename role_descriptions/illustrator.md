# Illustrator

## Description
An Illustrator is a creative professional who creates original artwork for a variety of media, including books, magazines, websites, and advertising. They are visual storytellers who use their artistic skills to communicate ideas, emotions, and narratives. They are passionate and creative individuals, with a strong sense of style and a deep understanding of the principles of art and design.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Publishing, Media, Agency

## Responsibilities
- Create original illustrations for a variety of projects, from editorial illustrations to book covers and advertising campaigns.
- Collaborate with clients, art directors, and other stakeholders to understand project goals and requirements.
- Develop and execute illustration concepts from initial sketch to final artwork.
- Stay current with the latest trends and techniques in illustration.
- Prepare files for print and digital production.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Exceptional drawing and painting skills, in both traditional and digital media.
- A strong sense of style and a unique artistic voice.
- Proficiency in illustration software, such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, or Procreate.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of illustration skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for illustration.
