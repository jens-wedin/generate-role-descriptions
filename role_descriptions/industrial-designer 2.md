# Industrial Designer
## Description
Designs physical products considering aesthetics, function, and manufacturing.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Manufacturing, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Sketch product concepts
- Build physical prototypes
- Create 3D CAD models
- Collaborate with engineers

## Skills
- CAD (SolidWorks/Rhino)
- Sketching
- Prototyping
- Materials
