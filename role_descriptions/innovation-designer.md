# Innovation Designer

## Description
An Innovation Designer is a creative and strategic thinker who uses design to imagine and create new products, services, and business models. They are experts in the fuzzy front end of the innovation process, from identifying unmet needs and opportunities to prototyping and testing new concepts. They are passionate about creating a better future and are comfortable working in a fast-paced and ambiguous environment.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Consulting, Enterprise

## Responsibilities
- Lead and facilitate the innovation process, from research and insights to concept development and prototyping.
- Conduct user research, market analysis, and trend forecasting to identify opportunities for innovation.
- Develop and visualize new product, service, and business model concepts.
- Create and test prototypes to validate and iterate on new ideas.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to bring new concepts to life.
- Communicate and evangelize a culture of innovation within the organization.

## Skills
- Expertise in design thinking, service design, and other innovation methodologies.
- Strong research, analysis, and synthesis skills.
- Excellent communication, facilitation, and storytelling skills.
- A passion for innovation and a creative mindset.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to think critically and creatively about the future.
- Ability to navigate ambiguity and uncertainty.
