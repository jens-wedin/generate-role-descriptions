# Innovation Lead

## Description
An Innovation Lead is a senior professional who is responsible for leading and guiding a team of innovators. They are a player-coach who is both a skilled innovation practitioner and a mentor to others. They are responsible for setting the vision and direction for the innovation team, and for ensuring that the team is delivering high-quality work that drives business growth.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Enterprise, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor a team of innovators, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Develop and implement an innovation strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Oversee the execution of innovation projects from concept to completion.
- Champion a culture of innovation throughout the organization.
- Collaborate with other teams to ensure that innovation is integrated into the entire product development process.
- Establish and track key innovation metrics to measure the success of the team's work.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in innovation.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Expertise in innovation methodologies, such as design thinking, lean startup, and open innovation.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and facilitation skills.
- A data-driven mindset and a focus on results.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing teams.
- A strong track record of success in leading innovation projects.
