# Innovation Manager

## Description
An Innovation Manager is a leader who is responsible for the overall innovation process and portfolio for a company. They are a coach, a mentor, and a facilitator, with a passion for helping the innovation team do their best work. They are responsible for creating a supportive and collaborative environment where the innovation team can learn, grow, and thrive.

## Org Level
Manager

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Enterprise

## Responsibilities
- Manage a team of innovators, including hiring, onboarding, performance reviews, and career development.
- Provide coaching, mentorship, and guidance to the innovation team.
- Foster a positive and inclusive team culture.
- Manage the team's workload and resources.
- Facilitate communication and collaboration within the innovation team and with other departments.
- Advocate for the needs of the innovation team.
- Continuously improve the innovation team's processes and workflows.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and people management skills.
- A deep understanding of the innovation process and the needs of innovators.
- Excellent coaching, mentoring, and facilitation skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- A passion for building and leading high-performing teams.
- Experience in an innovation role is often a plus.
