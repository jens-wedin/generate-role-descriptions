# Innovation Strategist

## Description
An Innovation Strategist is a forward-thinking professional who is responsible for developing and executing the innovation strategy for a company. They are experts in identifying and capitalizing on new growth opportunities, from new products and services to new business models and markets. They are analytical and creative thinkers, with a passion for using innovation to drive business results.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Consulting

## Responsibilities
- Develop and execute a comprehensive innovation strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Conduct research and analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and threats in the market.
- Develop and manage a portfolio of innovation projects.
- Identify and evaluate new and emerging technologies and business models.
- Collaborate with other teams to ensure that innovation is integrated into the entire product development process.
- Communicate the innovation strategy to stakeholders at all levels of the organization.

## Skills
- Expertise in innovation strategy and management.
- Strong analytical and strategic thinking skills.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
- A passion for innovation and a deep understanding of the latest trends and technologies.
- A strategic and creative mindset.
- Experience with a variety of innovation tools and methodologies.
