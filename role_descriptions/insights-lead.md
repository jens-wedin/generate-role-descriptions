# Insights Lead

## Description
An Insights Lead is a senior professional who is responsible for leading a team of researchers and analysts in the generation of consumer and market insights. They are a player-coach who is both a skilled researcher and a mentor to others. They are responsible for setting the vision and direction for the insights team, and for ensuring that the team is delivering high-quality work that drives business growth.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Marketing, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor a team of researchers and analysts, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Develop and implement a research and insights strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Oversee the execution of research projects from concept to completion.
- Synthesize data from a variety of sources to generate actionable insights.
- Communicate insights to stakeholders at all levels of the organization in a clear and compelling way.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in research and insights.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Expertise in a wide range of qualitative and quantitative research methods.
- Excellent analytical and synthesis skills.
- Strong communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- A passion for understanding consumers and a deep curiosity about the market.
- A strong track record of success in leading research and insights projects.
