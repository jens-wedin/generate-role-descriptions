# Service Design Lead

## Description
A Service Design Lead is a senior service design professional who is responsible for leading a team of service designers and for the overall quality of the service design work. They are a player-coach who is both a skilled service designer and a mentor to others. They are responsible for setting the vision and direction for the service design team, and for ensuring that the team is delivering high-quality work that meets the needs of both users and the business.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Service, Gov

## Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor a team of service designers, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Develop and implement a service design strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Oversee the execution of service design projects from concept to completion.
- Champion a human-centered and systems-thinking approach to service design.
- Collaborate with other teams to ensure that service design is integrated into the entire product development process.
- Establish and track key service design metrics to measure the success of the team's work.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in service design.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Expertise in service design, systems thinking, and user research.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and facilitation skills.
- A data-driven mindset and a focus on results.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing teams.
- A strong track record of success in leading service design projects.
