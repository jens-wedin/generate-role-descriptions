# Surface Designer

## Description
A Surface Designer is a creative professional who specializes in the design of patterns, textures, and other surface treatments for a wide range of products, from textiles and wallpaper to ceramics and stationery. They are masters of the two-dimensional canvas, with a deep understanding of color, pattern, and repeat. They are passionate about creating beautiful and unique surfaces that bring joy to everyday life.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Textiles, Interiors

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of surface patterns and textures.
- Collaborate with clients, art directors, and other stakeholders to understand project goals and requirements.
- Develop and execute surface design concepts from initial sketch to final production.
- Stay current with the latest trends and techniques in surface design.
- Prepare files for a variety of production processes, from digital printing to screen printing.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in the principles of pattern design, including repeat, color, and scale.
- Proficiency in design software, such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of surface design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for surface design.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
