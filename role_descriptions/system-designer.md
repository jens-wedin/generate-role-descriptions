# System Designer

## Description
A System Designer is a designer who specializes in the design of complex systems, such as game mechanics, economic models, or organizational processes. They are masters of complexity, with a deep understanding of how to design systems that are balanced, engaging, and resilient. They are passionate about creating systems that are both elegant and effective.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Gaming, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Design and document the rules, mechanics, and logic of complex systems.
- Create and balance the parameters of a system to achieve a desired outcome.
- Prototype and test systems to ensure that they are working as intended.
- Collaborate with other designers, engineers, and stakeholders to integrate systems into a larger product or experience.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in system design.
- Advocate for a systems-thinking approach to design.

## Skills
- A deep understanding of systems thinking and complexity theory.
- Expertise in a variety of modeling and simulation tools.
- Strong analytical and mathematical skills.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A passion for designing complex systems and a creative mindset.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to design and document complex systems.
