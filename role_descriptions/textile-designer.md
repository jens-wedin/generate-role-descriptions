# Textile Designer

## Description
A Textile Designer is a creative professional who specializes in the design of knitted, woven, and printed fabrics. They are responsible for creating the patterns, colors, and textures that we see in a wide range of products, from fashion and home furnishings to automotive interiors and medical textiles. They are masters of the loom and the print screen, with a deep understanding of materials, manufacturing processes, and the history of textile design.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Fashion, Interiors

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of textile designs, from initial sketch to final production.
- Collaborate with clients, art directors, and other stakeholders to understand project goals and requirements.
- Develop and execute textile design concepts from initial sketch to final production.
- Stay current with the latest trends and techniques in textile design.
- Prepare files for a variety of production processes, from digital printing to weaving.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in the principles of textile design, including pattern, color, and repeat.
- Proficiency in design software, such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, as well as specialized textile design software.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of textile design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for textiles.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
