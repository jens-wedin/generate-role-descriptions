# Transformation Lead

## Description
A Transformation Lead is a senior professional who is responsible for leading a team of transformation specialists and for the overall quality of the transformation work. They are a player-coach who is both a skilled transformation practitioner and a mentor to others. They are responsible for setting the vision and direction for the transformation team, and for ensuring that the team is delivering high-quality work that drives business growth.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Consulting

## Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor a team of transformation specialists, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Develop and implement a transformation strategy that is aligned with the company's business goals.
- Oversee the execution of transformation projects from concept to completion.
- Champion a culture of transformation throughout the organization.
- Collaborate with other teams to ensure that transformation is integrated into the entire product development process.
- Establish and track key transformation metrics to measure the success of the team's work.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in transformation.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Expertise in transformation methodologies, such as change management, business process reengineering, and organizational design.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and facilitation skills.
- A data-driven mindset and a focus on results.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing teams.
- A strong track record of success in leading transformation projects.
