# UI Lead

## Description
A UI (User Interface) Lead is a senior UI professional who is responsible for the UI design of a specific product, project, or team. They are a hands-on leader who is both a skilled UI designer and a mentor to other designers. They are responsible for ensuring that the UI work is aligned with the project's goals, and for guiding the team to create a high-quality, user-centered experience.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Lead the UI design of a specific product, project, or feature from concept to completion.
- Provide guidance, feedback, and mentorship to other UI designers on the team.
- Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and other stakeholders to ensure a cohesive and user-centered product.
- Facilitate design critiques and reviews to ensure a high standard of quality.
- Advocate for the user and for UI best practices.
- Contribute to the development of the UI team's culture and processes.

## Skills
- Expertise in UI design, visual design, and interaction design.
- Strong leadership and mentoring skills.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and presentation skills.
- A passion for creating high-quality, user-centered experiences.
- A strong portfolio that demonstrates a deep understanding of the UI design process and a track record of success.
- Ability to provide and receive constructive feedback.
