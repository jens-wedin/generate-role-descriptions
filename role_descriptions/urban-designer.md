# Urban Designer

## Description
An Urban Designer is a creative professional who specializes in the design of cities, towns, and other urban areas. They are responsible for creating places that are not only beautiful, but also functional, sustainable, and equitable. They are masters of the urban realm, with a deep understanding of urban planning, architecture, and landscape architecture. They are passionate about creating great cities for people.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Physical

## Industry
Urban Planning, Gov

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of urban design projects, from master plans to streetscape designs.
- Collaborate with community members, government agencies, and other stakeholders to understand the needs and aspirations of the community.
- Develop and execute urban design concepts from initial sketch to final implementation.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in urban design.
- Prepare and present design concepts to clients and the public.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in urban design, urban planning, and landscape architecture.
- Proficiency in design software, such as AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Adobe Creative Suite.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of urban design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and community engagement skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for creating great cities.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
