# Usability Researcher

## Description
A Usability Researcher is a user experience professional who specializes in the evaluation of the usability of products, services, and experiences. They are experts in a wide range of usability testing methods, and they use that knowledge to identify and prioritize usability issues. They are passionate about creating products that are easy and enjoyable to use, and they are skilled in a variety of research and analysis tools.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Plan and conduct a wide range of usability tests, from moderated and unmoderated tests to heuristic evaluations and cognitive walkthroughs.
- Analyze and synthesize usability data to identify and prioritize usability issues.
- Communicate usability findings and recommendations to designers, product managers, engineers, and other stakeholders in a clear and compelling way.
- Collaborate with other researchers and designers to create a comprehensive understanding of the user experience.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in usability testing.
- Advocate for a user-centered and iterative approach to design.

## Skills
- Expertise in a variety of usability testing methods.
- Strong analytical and synthesis skills.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and storytelling skills.
- A deep sense of empathy and a passion for understanding people.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to identify and communicate usability issues.
- Experience with a variety of research tools and technologies.
