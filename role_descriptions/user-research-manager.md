# User Research Manager

## Description
A User Research Manager is a leader who is responsible for the health, happiness, and performance of a team of user researchers. They are a coach, a mentor, and a facilitator, with a passion for helping user researchers do their best work. They are responsible for creating a supportive and collaborative environment where user researchers can learn, grow, and thrive.

## Org Level
Manager

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Manage a team of user researchers, including hiring, onboarding, performance reviews, and career development.
- Provide coaching, mentorship, and guidance to user researchers.
- Foster a positive and inclusive team culture.
- Manage the team's workload and resources.
- Facilitate communication and collaboration within the user research team and with other departments.
- Advocate for the needs of the user research team.
- Continuously improve the user research team's processes and workflows.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and people management skills.
- A deep understanding of the user research process and the needs of user researchers.
- Excellent coaching, mentoring, and facilitation skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- A passion for building and leading high-performing teams.
- Experience as a user researcher is often a plus.
