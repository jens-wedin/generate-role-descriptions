# UX Architect

## Description
A UX Architect is a user experience professional who specializes in the design of the underlying structure and flow of a digital product. They are responsible for creating a logical and intuitive user experience by focusing on the organization, navigation, and labeling of content. They are passionate about creating user experiences that are both easy and enjoyable to use.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Design and create the information architecture, navigation, and user flows for a digital product.
- Create wireframes, sitemaps, and other design artifacts to communicate the UX architecture.
- Collaborate with UX designers, UI designers, and other stakeholders to create a cohesive and user-centered product.
- Conduct user research and usability testing to validate and iterate on the UX architecture.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in UX architecture.
- Advocate for a user-centered and iterative approach to design.

## Skills
- A deep understanding of UX architecture principles and best practices.
- Expertise in a variety of design and prototyping tools, such as Figma, Sketch, or Axure.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A passion for creating intuitive and engaging user experiences.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to design and document complex UX architectures.
