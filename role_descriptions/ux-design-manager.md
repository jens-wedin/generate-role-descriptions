# UX Design Manager

## Description
A UX Design Manager is a leader who is responsible for the health, happiness, and performance of a team of UX designers. They are a coach, a mentor, and a facilitator, with a passion for helping UX designers do their best work. They are responsible for creating a supportive and collaborative environment where UX designers can learn, grow, and thrive.

## Org Level
Manager

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Manage a team of UX designers, including hiring, onboarding, performance reviews, and career development.
- Provide coaching, mentorship, and guidance to UX designers.
- Foster a positive and inclusive team culture.
- Manage the team's workload and resources.
- Facilitate communication and collaboration within the UX design team and with other departments.
- Advocate for the needs of the UX design team.
- Continuously improve the UX design team's processes and workflows.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and people management skills.
- A deep understanding of the UX design process and the needs of UX designers.
- Excellent coaching, mentoring, and facilitation skills.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- A passion for building and leading high-performing teams.
- Experience as a UX designer is often a plus.
