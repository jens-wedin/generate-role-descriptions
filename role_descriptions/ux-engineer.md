# UX Engineer

## Description
A UX Engineer is a front-end developer who specializes in the implementation of user interfaces with a focus on user experience. They are a hybrid of a designer and a developer, with a deep understanding of both design principles and front-end technologies. They are passionate about creating user interfaces that are not only beautiful and functional, but also performant, accessible, and easy to use.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Translate design mockups and prototypes into high-quality, production-ready code.
- Build and maintain reusable UI components and design systems.
- Collaborate with designers to create and prototype new user experiences.
- Ensure that the front-end of the product is accessible, performant, and responsive.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in front-end development and UX.
- Act as a bridge between the design and engineering teams, helping to foster a collaborative and user-centered culture.

## Skills
- Expertise in front-end technologies, such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Experience with modern JavaScript frameworks, such as React, Vue, or Angular.
- A strong understanding of design principles, including typography, color, and layout.
- Proficiency in design and prototyping tools, such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.
- A passion for creating beautiful and user-friendly interfaces.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong ability to bridge the gap between design and engineering.
