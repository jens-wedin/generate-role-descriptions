# UX Strategist

## Description
A UX Strategist is a senior user experience professional who is responsible for aligning the user experience with the overall business strategy. They are a big-picture thinker who is passionate about creating user experiences that are not only user-friendly, but also drive business results. They are experts in understanding the needs of both users and the business, and in creating a UX strategy that meets those needs.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech, Agency

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate a clear and compelling UX strategy for a product, a business unit, or a company.
- Conduct user research, market analysis, and competitive analysis to identify opportunities for innovation.
- Collaborate with senior leaders in product, engineering, and marketing to ensure that the UX strategy is aligned with the overall business strategy.
- Create and maintain a UX roadmap that prioritizes the most important UX initiatives.
- Measure and track the impact of the UX strategy on key business metrics.
- Advocate for a user-centered and data-informed approach to design.

## Skills
- A deep understanding of UX strategy and how it relates to business strategy.
- Expertise in a variety of research and strategy frameworks.
- Strong analytical and strategic thinking skills.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and influencing skills.
- A passion for creating user experiences that drive business results.
- A portfolio that demonstrates a strong ability to think strategically about UX.
