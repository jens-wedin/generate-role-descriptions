# UX/UI Lead

## Description
A UX/UI Lead is a senior design professional who is responsible for the UX and UI design of a specific product, project, or team. They are a hands-on leader who is both a skilled UX/UI designer and a mentor to other designers. They are responsible for ensuring that the UX/UI work is aligned with the project's goals, and for guiding the team to create a high-quality, user-centered experience.

## Org Level
Craft Lead

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Lead the UX/UI design of a specific product, project, or feature from concept to completion.
- Provide guidance, feedback, and mentorship to other UX/UI designers on the team.
- Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and other stakeholders to ensure a cohesive and user-centered product.
- Facilitate design critiques and reviews to ensure a high standard of quality.
- Advocate for the user and for UX/UI best practices.
- Contribute to the development of the UX/UI team's culture and processes.

## Skills
- Expertise in UX and UI design, as well as in user research and interaction design.
- Strong leadership and mentoring skills.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and presentation skills.
- A passion for creating high-quality, user-centered experiences.
- A strong portfolio that demonstrates a deep understanding of the UX/UI design process and a track record of success.
- Ability to provide and receive constructive feedback.
