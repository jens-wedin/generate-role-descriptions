# Vibe Coder

## Description
A Vibe Coder is a creative coder who specializes in the creation of atmospheric and emotionally resonant experiences. They are a hybrid of a programmer and an artist, with a deep understanding of how to use code to create mood, feeling, and a sense of place. They are passionate about using technology to create immersive and evocative experiences that go beyond the purely functional.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Music, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop interactive and generative art installations.
- Create real-time visual effects for music, performance, and other live events.
- Collaborate with artists, musicians, and other creatives to bring their visions to life.
- Experiment with new and emerging technologies to create novel and innovative experiences.
- Stay current with the latest trends and techniques in creative coding and generative art.

## Skills
- Expertise in a variety of creative coding frameworks and languages, such as TouchDesigner, Processing, or openFrameworks.
- A strong sense of aesthetics and a passion for creating beautiful and evocative experiences.
- Excellent problem-solving and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for experimentation.
- A portfolio that showcases a strong ability to use code to create atmospheric and emotionally resonant experiences.
