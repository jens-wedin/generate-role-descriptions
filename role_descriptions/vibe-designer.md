# Vibe Designer

## Description
A Vibe Designer is a creative professional who specializes in the curation of the atmosphere and mood of a space or experience. They are a hybrid of a designer, a curator, and a DJ, with a deep understanding of how to use sensory inputs to create a desired emotional response. They are passionate about creating immersive and memorable experiences that transport people to another place.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Events, Hospitality

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of sensory experiences, from music playlists and lighting designs to scent profiles and tactile installations.
- Collaborate with clients, event planners, and other stakeholders to understand the desired mood and atmosphere.
- Develop and execute a cohesive sensory strategy for a space or experience.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in sensory design.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in the principles of sensory design.
- A deep understanding of how to use music, lighting, scent, and other sensory inputs to create a desired emotional response.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of sensory design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for creating immersive experiences.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
