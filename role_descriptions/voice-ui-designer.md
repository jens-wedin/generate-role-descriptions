# Voice UI Designer

## Description
A Voice UI Designer is a user experience professional who specializes in the design of voice-based interactions. They are responsible for creating voice user interfaces (VUIs) that are not only natural and intuitive, but also efficient and enjoyable to use. They are masters of the spoken word, with a deep understanding of linguistics, conversation design, and the nuances of human speech.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Sound

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Design and create the voice user interface for a wide range of products, from smart speakers and mobile apps to in-car assistants and interactive voice response (IVR) systems.
- Collaborate with UX designers, product managers, and engineers to create a cohesive and user-centered product.
- Create and maintain a voice and tone guide to ensure consistency and quality across all products.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in voice UI design.
- Prepare and deliver VUI specifications to the engineering team.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in the principles of voice UI design, including conversation design, persona design, and error handling.
- Proficiency in a variety of VUI design and prototyping tools.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of VUI design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for voice.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
