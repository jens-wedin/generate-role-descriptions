# VP of Design

## Description
A VP of Design is a senior executive who is responsible for the overall design vision, strategy, and execution for a company. They are a visionary and a mentor, with a passion for building and leading high-performing design teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for design excellence and for ensuring that the design team is creating work that is both beautiful and effective. They are a key member of the executive team and play a critical role in shaping the company's strategy and culture.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Tech, Enterprise

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the design vision and strategy for the company.
- Lead and mentor a team of design directors and managers, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the design of all products, services, and experiences, ensuring a high standard of quality and consistency.
- Collaborate with other executives to ensure that design is integrated into the entire business.
- Champion a user-centered design culture throughout the company.
- Attract, hire, and retain top design talent.
- Manage the design budget and resources.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Deep expertise in a variety of design disciplines, such as UX design, product design, or brand design.
- A compelling portfolio of work that demonstrates a commitment to design excellence.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing design teams.
