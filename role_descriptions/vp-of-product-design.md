# VP of Product Design

## Description
A VP of Product Design is a senior executive who is responsible for the overall product design vision, strategy, and execution for a company. They are a visionary and a mentor, with a passion for building and leading high-performing product design teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for product design excellence and for ensuring that the product design team is creating products that are both user-friendly and valuable to the business. They are a key member of the executive team and play a critical role in shaping the company's product strategy.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the product design vision and strategy for the company.
- Lead and mentor a team of product design directors and managers, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the design of all products, ensuring a high standard of quality, consistency, and user-friendliness.
- Collaborate with other executives to ensure that product design is integrated into the entire business.
- Champion a user-centered and data-informed design culture throughout the company.
- Attract, hire, and retain top product design talent.
- Manage the product design budget and resources.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Deep expertise in product design, UX design, and user research.
- A compelling portfolio of work that demonstrates a commitment to product design excellence.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing product design teams.
