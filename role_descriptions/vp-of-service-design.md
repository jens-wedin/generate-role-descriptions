# VP of Service Design

## Description
A VP of Service Design is a senior executive who is responsible for the overall service design vision, strategy, and execution for a company. They are a visionary and a mentor, with a passion for building and leading high-performing service design teams. They are responsible for setting the bar for service design excellence and for ensuring that the service design team is creating end-to-end service experiences that are both user-friendly and valuable to the business. They are a key member of the executive team and play a critical role in shaping the company's strategy and culture.

## Org Level
Strategic

## Medium
Service

## Industry
Service, Finance

## Responsibilities
- Develop and articulate the service design vision and strategy for the company.
- Lead and mentor a team of service design directors and managers, providing guidance, feedback, and career development.
- Oversee the design of all service experiences, ensuring a high standard of quality, consistency, and user-friendliness.
- Collaborate with other executives to ensure that service design is integrated into the entire business.
- Champion a human-centered and systems-thinking approach to service design.
- Attract, hire, and retain top service design talent.
- Manage the service design budget and resources.

## Skills
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Deep expertise in service design, systems thinking, and user research.
- A compelling portfolio of work that demonstrates a commitment to service design excellence.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills.
- Strong strategic thinking and business acumen.
- A passion for building and mentoring high-performing service design teams.
