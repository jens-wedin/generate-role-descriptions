# Web Designer

## Description
A Web Designer is a creative professional who specializes in the design of websites. They are responsible for creating websites that are not only visually appealing, but also user-friendly, accessible, and optimized for search engines. They are masters of the web, with a deep understanding of web design principles, front-end technologies, and the ever-changing landscape of the internet.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Agency, Tech

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of websites, from simple landing pages to complex e-commerce sites.
- Collaborate with clients, developers, and other stakeholders to understand the goals and requirements of the website.
- Develop and execute web design concepts from initial wireframe to final implementation.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in web design.
- Prepare and deliver design assets to the development team.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in the principles of web design, including responsive design, accessibility, and SEO.
- Proficiency in design and prototyping tools, such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of web design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for the web.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
