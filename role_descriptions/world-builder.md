# World Builder

## Description
A World Builder is a creative professional who specializes in the design and creation of fictional worlds. They are responsible for creating the lore, history, geography, and culture of a world, as well as the characters and creatures that inhabit it. They are masters of imagination, with a deep understanding of storytelling, mythology, and the principles of world design. They are passionate about creating worlds that are not only believable, but also immersive and engaging.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Gaming, Metaverse

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of fictional worlds, from high fantasy to science fiction.
- Collaborate with writers, artists, and other creatives to bring the world to life.
- Develop and execute a cohesive and consistent vision for the world.
- Stay current with the latest trends and best practices in world building.
- Prepare and present world-building materials to clients and internal stakeholders.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in the principles of world building, including history, geography, and culture.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of world-building skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for storytelling.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
- A deep and wide-ranging knowledge of history, mythology, and a variety of cultures.
