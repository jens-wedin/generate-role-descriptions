# XR Designer

## Description
An XR Designer is a creative professional who specializes in the design of extended reality (XR) experiences, including virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and mixed reality (MR). They are responsible for creating immersive and interactive experiences that blend the physical and digital worlds. They are masters of the spatial realm, with a deep understanding of 3D design, interaction design, and the psychology of presence. They are passionate about creating XR experiences that are not only beautiful, but also believable and engaging.

## Org Level
IC

## Medium
Digital

## Industry
Tech

## Responsibilities
- Design and create a wide range of XR experiences, from games and simulations to training and social applications.
- Collaborate with artists, programmers, and other team members to bring XR worlds to life.
- Develop and execute XR design concepts from initial sketch to final implementation.
- Stay current with the latest trends and techniques in XR design.
- Prepare and present design concepts to clients and internal stakeholders.
- Manage multiple projects and deadlines.

## Skills
- Expertise in the principles of XR design, including immersion, presence, and interaction.
- Proficiency in 3D modeling and game engines, such as Unity or Unreal Engine.
- A strong portfolio that showcases a range of XR design skills and a commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
- A creative mindset and a passion for XR.
- Meticulous attention to detail.
